Nostalgia Program: 1970s / RnB Soul
1970S RNB SOUL
45 tracks • 25 artists • 1970–1997

R&B / Soul is emotion in high fidelity—gospel‑taught phrasing, sensual grooves, and melodic storytelling. The 1950s and 1960s brought church to the charts; the 1970s layered strings and grit; the 1980s soared on powerhouse vocals and drum‑machine precision; the 1990s married harmonies to hip‑hop swing; the 2000s kept R&B near pop’s center; the 2010s explored minimalism and alt‑soul palettes; and the 2020s stretch from throwback warmth to futuristic shimmer. Through every turn, the genre centers feeling—love, longing, and lift. For decade‑specific standouts and innovators, check the decade‑genre sections.
