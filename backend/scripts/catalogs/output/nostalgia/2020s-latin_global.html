Nostalgia Program: 2020s / Latin Global
2020S LATIN GLOBAL
45 tracks • 22 artists • 2020–2023

Latin Global is the sound of continents in conversation. Mid‑century mambo, bolero, and bossa carried elegance worldwide; ’70s salsa electrified city nights; ’80s and ’90s pop crossovers brought bilingual hooks to the mainstream; 2000s reggaetón and rock en español cemented rhythmic swagger; the 2010s pushed urbano to the global forefront; and the 2020s keep widening the circle with regional Mexican, dembow, and beyond. It’s percussion, melody, and identity—danced, sung, streamed, and shared across borders. For who defined each era’s pulse, see the decade‑genre deep dives.
