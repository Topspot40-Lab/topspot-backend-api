Nostalgia Program: 2000s / Folk Acoustic
2000S FOLK ACOUSTIC
45 tracks • 6 artists • 2004–2009

Folk / Acoustic is storytelling at arm’s length—the creak of wood, the breath between lines. The 1950s revived ballads and traditional forms; the 1960s made the coffeehouse a campus for protest and poetry; the 1970s turned introspection into platinum songcraft; the 1980s/1990s kept the flame alive in indie corners; the 2000s and 2010s sparked sing‑along revivals; and the 2020s blend diaristic bedroom recordings with festival catharsis. Stripped arrangements keep the lyric in focus. For how each decade gave folk its flavor—and which writers lit the path—see the decade‑genre details.
