Nostalgia Program: 1970s / pop
1970S POP
45 tracks • 30 artists • 1972–1982

Pop is the big‑tent promise that a melody can unite a room. Its engine is immediacy: verse‑pre‑chorus‑chorus and an emotional truth delivered with polish. From 1950s crooners and girl‑group harmonies, to 1960s studio wizardry, 1970s glamour and soft‑rock ease, 1980s synth sheen and video spectacle, 1990s teen‑pop hooks, 2000s global collaborations, 2010s intimate confessionals, and 2020s hybrid beats—pop always mirrors the broader culture. Technology shapes it (from four‑tracks to phones), as do fashion, film, and dance. If a song is the year’s signature selfie, pop is the front camera. For the stars and sounds that defined each decade, see the decade‑genre breakdowns.
