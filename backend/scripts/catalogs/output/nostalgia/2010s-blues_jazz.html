Nostalgia Program: 2010s / Blues Jazz
2010S BLUES JAZZ
43 tracks • 23 artists • 2010–2022

Blues / Jazz is the living memory of American music—call‑and‑response turned into language. The 1950s polished LP artistry without losing club‑room grit; the 1960s balanced modal exploration with civil‑rights urgency; the 1970s fused funk, rock, and improvisation; the 1980s alternated between smooth textures and acoustic revival; the 1990s taught history alongside crossover radio; the 2000s and 2010s stretched into film scores, festivals, and viral solos; and the 2020s thrive on collaboration. The groove is a conversation that never ends. For decade‑by‑decade voices and landmarks, see the combo entries.
