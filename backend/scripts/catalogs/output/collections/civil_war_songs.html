Collection Group: American Heritage Favorites
CIVIL WAR SONGS

A collection of historic songs that tell the stories of soldiers, families, sacrifice, and perseverance during one of the most important periods in American history.

Featured Artists

Tennessee Ernie Ford (6) Bobby Horton (5) The 2nd South Carolina String Band (4) Mitch Miller (2) Robert Shaw Chorale (2) Bing Crosby (2) Paul Robeson (2) Johnny Cash (2)
